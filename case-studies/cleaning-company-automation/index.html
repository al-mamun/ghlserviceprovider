From Manual Chaos to Full Automation — A Cleaning Company's Story

The Situation

Sarah runs a residential cleaning company with 6 cleaners and about 80 regular clients. By most measures, she had a successful business. Good reputation, steady repeat customers, reliable team.

But every morning before her cleaners arrived, she spent 2–3 hours on admin — confirming bookings, sending reminders, following up on enquiries, chasing invoices, replying to messages. Every evening, another hour or so doing the same.

She came to us not because her business was failing — it was fine. She came because she was exhausted and couldn't grow without either working longer hours or hiring someone just to do admin. Neither felt right.

What Was Eating Her Time

When we audited her workflow, we found the same 5 tasks being done manually every single day:

  • Confirming upcoming bookings with clients via WhatsApp
  • Sending reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before each clean
  • Following up with enquiries that hadn't booked yet
  • Asking happy clients for Google reviews after a clean
  • Sending re-booking reminders to clients who hadn't booked their next clean

None of these required human judgment. Every single one could be automated perfectly. But nobody had ever set it up.

What We Built

Booking Confirmation System

Every booking now triggers an immediate confirmation SMS and email. The client gets a personalised message with the date, time, cleaner's name and a link to manage or reschedule if needed. Sarah does nothing.

Reminder Sequence

24 hours before each clean: SMS reminder with all the details and a one-click reschedule link. 2 hours before: a short, friendly reminder. No-shows dropped from roughly 3–4 per month to near zero.

Review Automation

4 hours after each completed clean, the client receives an SMS: "Hi [name]! Hope everything looks spotless ✨ If you're happy, we'd love a quick Google review — it really helps us: [link]"

In the first 60 days, 38 new Google reviews came in. Her rating went from 4.1 (with 19 reviews) to 4.9 (with 57 reviews). Her Google Maps ranking for "cleaning company [city]" moved from page 2 to the top 3 results.

Re-Booking Automation

Clients who haven't rebooked within 5 weeks get a gentle nudge: "Hi [name]! It's been a little while — is it time for another clean? We have availability this week. Here's our booking link: [link]"

This alone recovered 7–8 clients per month who had simply drifted without anyone following up.

New Enquiry Follow-Up

Every new enquiry gets an instant response, a follow-up after 24 hours if they haven't booked, and a final message after 72 hours. The conversion rate from enquiry to booking went from 31% to 54%.

The Outcome After 90 Days

Daily Admin Time3+ hours→Under 20 minutes
Google Reviews19 reviews, 4.1★→57 reviews, 4.9★
Enquiry Conversion31%→54%
Monthly No-Shows3–4/month→Near zero
Recovered Lapsed Clients0 (nobody chased)→7–8/month

Three months later, Sarah hired a second cleaner — not to handle admin, but because the business had grown enough to need one. The time she saved on manual work went into actually growing the business, not maintaining it.

In her words: "I didn't realise how much of my day I was spending on things that didn't need me. Now I focus on the stuff only I can do, and everything else just runs."
