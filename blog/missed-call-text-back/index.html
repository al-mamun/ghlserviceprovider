Missed Call Text Back: How to Recover Every Lost Lead Automatically

By GHL Service Provider · May 18, 2026 · 5 min read

Every missed call is a missed sale. Without an instant follow-up, that caller hangs up and calls your competitor within minutes. GoHighLevel's Missed Call Text Back feature fixes this automatically — here's how to set it up in under 10 minutes.

📊 The hard truth: Studies show 78% of customers buy from the first business that responds. If you miss a call and don't follow up within 5 minutes, you have a 10x lower chance of reaching that lead.

What Is Missed Call Text Back?

Missed Call Text Back is a GoHighLevel automation that detects when an inbound call goes unanswered and immediately sends an automated SMS to that caller — usually within 30–60 seconds.

Instead of the caller hanging up and calling your competitor, they receive a text that keeps them engaged with your business while you're busy.

Step-by-Step Setup in GoHighLevel

Step 1: Enable the Feature

Go to Settings → Business Profile → Missed Call Text Back. Toggle it on. You'll see a default message already there — we'll customise this in a moment.

Step 2: Write Your Message

The default message is generic. Replace it with something personal and action-oriented. Here's a template that works well:

Hi {{contact.first_name}}! Sorry we missed your call. We'd love to help — reply to this message or click here to book a quick call at a time that works for you: [your booking link]

Keep it short, friendly and give them an easy next step. The goal is to keep the conversation going.

Step 3: Create a Follow-Up Workflow

The text back is just the start. Build a workflow that continues the conversation if they don't reply:

  1. Trigger: Missed Call
  2. Action 1: Send SMS (immediate)
  3. Wait: 2 hours
  4. Condition: If no reply received
  5. Action 2: Send follow-up SMS — "Just checking in! We have availability this week. Would any of these times work for a quick call?"
  6. Wait: 24 hours
  7. Action 3: Send final email follow-up

Step 4: Set Business Hours

Under Settings → Business Hours, set your operating hours. Missed call texts can still go out 24/7, but you can configure different messages for after-hours calls.

💡 After-hours tip: For calls outside business hours, send: "Thanks for calling! We're closed right now but open again at 9am. Reply here and we'll call you first thing."

Real Results From Missed Call Text Back

One of our clients — a local plumbing company — was missing 15–20 calls per week. After setting up missed call text back with a 3-step follow-up sequence, they recovered an average of 8 leads per week that would have been permanently lost.

At their average job value of $450, that's $3,600+ in recovered revenue every week from a feature that took 20 minutes to set up.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Generic message — "We'll call you back soon" doesn't give them a next step
  • No booking link — always include a way for them to take action immediately
  • Only one message — most leads need 2–3 touchpoints before responding
  • Wrong timing — make sure your timezone is set correctly in GHL settings
